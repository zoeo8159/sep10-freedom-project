Context

What is mental health & what are we doing to help it? Mental health is something that tells us where one stands mentally. Mental health can be good or can be good. Mental health is what tells us our well-being. This affects the way we act, think & feel. We must take care of it.

Hardware

Most apps are very helpful. Many consist of giving daily affirmations & reminders for meditations & mediations. These apps are easy to handle & most of the time affordable. Talkspace, Quit That!, & Headspace are great apps to use if you feel that you need support or somewhere to vent.

Model

There are many apps that can help with mental health. Many of these only provide advice & daily affirmations but can’t really do more than that. This new & improved mental health echo dot is a speaker that talks back to you & asks questions such as “how was your day, how are you feeling currently, what would cheer you up” & gives advice & a therapy session all in one. You are able to call others through it in case you don't feel like talking to the speaker & would rather talk to a loved one. Some other features are playing relaxing music, operating 24/7, collecting information so that it has personal recommendations for you, and putting timers to receive daily or hourly affirmations. This echo dot also offers solutions for some problems/gives recommendations. In case of an emergency you can set up a safe word & will call emergency contacts & emergency hotline. Something this echo dot does & other can’t is that it can scan your mood & keeps track of how long you’ve been free of an addiction.
